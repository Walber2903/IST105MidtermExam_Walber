Explore Cultural Connections: Vancouver & Recife

Discover the unique experiences of those moving from Recife, Brazil, to Vancouver, Canada, and how they navigate the cultural differences, joys, and challenges.

Start Exploring

Top Cultural Shocks

1. The Weather

Moving from the tropical warmth of Recife to the cool, rainy climate of Vancouver can be a shock! Vancouver’s grey skies are a big contrast to Recife’s sunshine.

2. Cultural Reserve

In Vancouver, people tend to be more reserved compared to the warmth and openness of Brazilians. This can take some time to get used to.

What People Miss Most from Recife

1. The Food

From tapioca to acarajé, the rich and flavorful foods of Recife are irreplaceable. While Vancouver has amazing international food, nothing compares to the flavors of home.

2. The People

Brazilians are known for their close-knit communities and warm social culture. Many people miss the sense of connection and family gatherings back in Recife.

Amazing Things About Vancouver

1. Natural Beauty

Vancouver’s breathtaking landscapes, from mountains to oceans, provide endless opportunities for outdoor activities. The city's natural beauty often surprises new arrivals from Brazil.

2. Cultural Diversity

Vancouver is a melting pot of cultures. The multicultural environment makes it easier to meet people from all over the world and feel at home.

My Personal Goal

At this stage of my life, my primary objective is to complete my college degree and build a successful career. I aim to secure a fulfilling job that aligns with my skills and passion. Eventually, I plan to immigrate to Canada and settle in a country that offers endless opportunities for growth and development.
